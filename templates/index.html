Football Player Comparison Tool

This tool allows you to look at players performance throughout the football season. This tool uses an API to gather information and displays it free of charge to the user.

Enter two player's names in the red box to compare the two players. This will allow the user to visualize their metrics and performance through the season. You may also select which metrics you want to see in your graph.

Enter a player name in the blue box to view their player profile. This will allow you to see an individual players stats and metrics in much more detail than the comparison tool.
